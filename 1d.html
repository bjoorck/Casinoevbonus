General Principles

  • 1NT and 2NT responses always show 5+ clubs
  • 1NT = 5–10 HCP; 2NT = 11–12 HCP
  • Both deny any 4-card major and 3+♦
  • 2♦ = 5–9 HCP, exactly 3♦, 4+ clubs, denies 4M
  • 3♦ = 4–9 HCP, 4+♦, denies 4M
  • 2♣ is game forcing unless responder rebids clubs; denies 4 spades
  • 2♥/2♠ are weak six-card preempts
  • Jump raises in ♦ are preemptive
  • Reverse bids show extra values
  • Opener’s NT rebids define strength ranges
  • 2NT/3NT after 1♥ response promise 2–3 spades with AQ/KJx or better
  • After 1♦–1♥–2♣: 2♦/2♥ = minimum non-forcing, 2♠ = GF ask spade stop, 2NT = min 4414 type, 3x = GF
