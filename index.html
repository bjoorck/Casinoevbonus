Maximize Your Expected Value Using Casino Bonuses

Introduction

Casino bonuses may look like free money — they’re not. The value you get depends on how you play.

True +EV (expected value) bonuses are rare in today’s strict online casino environment, but you can still make the most of the promotions you find. This guide covers practical strategies to increase your expected value (EV) from normal bonuses, non-sticky bonuses, and free spins.

We skip complicated math and focus on attack and grind phase, plus which slots work best in each phase.

Even with a disciplined strategy, losses will happen, as outcomes are ultimately random. The goal is smart, strategic play that maximizes the value from available bonuses.

Bonus Info: What to Look For

Before you start using any strategy, it’s important to understand how casino bonuses actually work. Terms and conditions can make or break the value of a promotion.

  • The best offers are usually 100% deposit bonuses with a 35× wagering requirement (35× bonus only, not 35× deposit+bonus).
  • A 100% bonus with 35× (deposit+bonus) wagering requirement is still better than no bonus at all.
  • Lower wagering requirements make it easier to clear, while higher bonus percentages give more expected value (EV).
  • Always check T&Cs such as eligible slots, max stake, wagering requirement.

Step-by-Step Formula for Normal Casino Bonuses

Bonus with wagering requirement

  • Step 1 — Small Deposit
    • Deposit €20–€50 (not the full bonus limit, even if €100+ is offered).
    • A smaller deposit makes the wagering requirement more manageable.
    • Once your deposit is in, move to step 2 - the Attack Phase.
  • Step 2 — Attack Phase (High Volatility)
    • This is where you aim for a big bankroll boost before grinding out the wagering.
    • Bet 60–100% of the maximum bet allowed for your bonus per spin to maximize potential wins during this phase.
    • Focus on high-volatility slots for a chance at a big win.
    • Stay in this phase until your balance is either:
      • €0 → If you bust, stop here. Only consider a new bonus if your bankroll comfortably allows it — never chase losses.
      • €500+ → If you reach €500+, move to Step 3, the Grind Phase.
    • Reality check: 8–9 out of 10 times, you’ll lose your deposit in the Attack Phase.

    Recommended attack slots (verify bonus eligibility first):

    • Jack and the Beanstalk — High Volatility, 96.3% RTP
    • Book of Dead — High Volatility, 96.21% RTP
    • Bonanza — High Volatility, 96.0% RTP
    • Dead or Alive II — Very High Volatility, 96.8% RTP
  • Step 3 — Grind Phase (Low Volatility)
    • Now that you’ve reached the Grind Phase, the focus shifts to low-volatility, high-RTP slots.
    • The goal is to clear wagering requirements without big swings.
    • Some casinos bonus rules prevent reducing your stake after a win. To stay safe, I always continue using the same stake I had in the Attack Phase throughout the Grind Phase.

    Recommended grind slots (verify bonus eligibility first):

    • Secrets of Atlantis — Low Volatility, 96.3% RTP
    • Starburst — Low Volatility, 96.1% RTP
    • Immortal Romance — Low Volatility, 96.86% RTP
    • Thunderstruck II — Low Volatility, 96.65% RTP

    Most of the time, you’ll be able to grind out the wagering requirement, but not always. Good luck!

Non-Sticky Bonuses

Non-sticky bonuses allow you to forfeit the bonus and withdraw your real money. With a non-sticky bonus, you always play your real money first.

Key Difference:

If you hit a win with real money in the attack phase, you can forfeit the bonus and withdraw your real money.

Free Spin Bonuses

Free Spin Bonuses work very similarly to non-sticky bonuses. The essential rule is: always play your real money first before using free spins. This way, if you hit a win with your real money, you can withdraw your winnings.

Key Difference:

The essential rule to increase your EV is: always play your real money first before using free spins.

If you hit a win with real money in the attack phase, you can forfeit the freespins and withdraw your winnings.
